#### Reception

You can check in here with a kiosk. Please note that attendees without a name badge may be denied entry due to venue policy, so please be sure to check in when entering the venue.

You will receive Ubuntu and UbuCon Korea 2025-related stickers, and you can purchase paid merchandise that will be unveiled for the first time at the event.

### 12th Floor

From 11:50 AM to 12:50 PM only, you can eat your lunch at 12th floor.

Due to venue policy, you should not use trash can installed by venue. Organizers and volunteers will set up an another trash bag for participants.


## Getting to the venue

There is no on-site parking available at the venue, so please use public transportation if possible.

## Health and Safety information

To provide safe environment for every participants, We require every participants to read and follow our Code of Conducts ([See *About* page for details](/en/about)). Waering nametags are also required to enter conference area, participate workshop that required seperate registration as well as social activities such as conference dinner and group tour. For people without nametags, We may ask to leave or deny entrance.

### Emergency Calls

- [Police: 112](tel:112)
- [Fire/Ambulance: 119](tel:119)
- [Travel Helpline: 1330](tel:1330)
